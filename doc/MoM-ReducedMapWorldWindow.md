Minimap_Coords()

takes world center x,y and map w,h
calcs top left map x,y

world center x - half map w
world center y - half map h

world wrap

e.g.,
    map w,h is 58,30
    map x, y is 18,11
    world mid x,y is 24,16

24 - 58 / 2 = 24 - 29 = -5 + 60 = 55
16 - 30 / 2 = 16 - 15 =  1

*minimap_x = 0, *minimap_y = 1
